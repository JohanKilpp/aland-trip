Kastelholm Castle
Sight ⭐
Åland's only medieval castle, first mentioned in 1388. Swedish kings including Gustav Vasa stayed here. Mad King Eric XIV was imprisoned in the Kuretorn keep. Jan Karlsgården Open Air Museum next door — same ticket, 19th-century Åland farm life. English guided tours weekends at 2pm in summer.
⏰ 10am–5pm📍 25km east🏚 Jan Karlsgården same ticket
Navigate here Castle website
🏚
Bomarsund Fortress Ruins
Free
Russian fortress bombed by an Anglo-French fleet in the Crimean War, 1854. 870-hectare ruins to explore freely. Modern visitor centre with virtual history. Shallow beach for a swim. Hiking on Prästö island. Perfect picnic spot.
💶 Free⏰ Open 24h🧺 Perfect picnic spot🏖 Beach nearby

Åland Museum + Art Museum
Afternoon
Two museums in one building — one ticket. 7,500 years of Åland history plus the art museum with local artists. Open year-round. Good wind-down after Bomarsund.

Kobba Klintar boat trip (optional)
Optional
If your ferry is afternoon, take a morning boat to Kobba Klintar — tiny pilot station island offshore. Old pilot house now a café with great Åland pancakes. Harmless snake colony on the island. Giant cruise ships pass within metres. ~2h round trip from Mariehamn western harbour.
